Privacy Policy - AItist

Effective Date: June 9, 2025
Last Updated: June 9, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how AItist ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). AItist is an AI-powered photo styling application that allows users to transform their photos using artificial intelligence.

Information We Collect

1. Photos and Images

  • What we collect: Photos you select from your device's photo library for style transformation
  • How we use it: To apply AI style transformations and create styled versions of your images
  • Storage: Original images are temporarily stored on our servers for processing only and automatically deleted within 1 hour after processing
  • Final images: Styled images are stored permanently on our servers until you request deletion

2. Purchase and Subscription Data

  • What we collect: Purchase history, subscription status, credit balance, and transaction information
  • How we use it: To manage your subscription, track credit usage, and provide purchase-related features
  • Storage: Stored securely on our servers and synced with Apple's StoreKit system
  • Third-party sharing: Shared with Apple for payment processing and subscription management

3. App Account Tokens (StoreKit 2)

  • What we collect: Unique tokens generated by Apple for purchase validation
  • How we use it: To securely validate purchases and prevent fraud
  • Storage: Processed temporarily and not permanently stored

4. Authentication Information

  • What we collect: Authentication tokens for app functionality
  • How we use it: To provide access to AI styling features and maintain your app session
  • Storage: Securely stored using iOS Keychain on your device

5. Device Information

  • What we collect: Device UUID for app functionality
  • How we use it: To maintain app state and provide personalized experience
  • Storage: Stored locally using iOS Keychain

6. Usage and Analytics Information

  • What we collect: App usage patterns, feature interactions, and performance metrics
  • How we use it: To improve app performance, user experience, and service reliability
  • Storage: Processed locally and aggregated on our servers (anonymized)

7. Network Communication Data

  • What we collect: API requests, response times, and connection status
  • How we use it: To ensure service reliability and troubleshoot technical issues
  • Storage: Temporarily logged for debugging purposes, then deleted after 7 days

Device Permissions

Photo Library Access

  • Reading permission: To select existing photos for style transformation
  • Writing permission: To save styled images back to your photo library
  • User control: You can manage these permissions in iOS Settings
  • Note: We do not request camera access - you can only select existing photos from your library

Data Processing and AI

Local Processing

  • All AI style transformations are processed through secure API calls to OpenAI
  • Your original photos are temporarily stored on our servers for processing only
  • Original images are automatically deleted within 1 hour after processing
  • Styled images are generated and stored on our servers for download

Third-Party AI Services

  • We use OpenAI's image editing services for AI style transformation
  • Original images and user prompts are sent to OpenAI for processing
  • OpenAI may retain images for up to 30 days according to their data usage policy
  • OpenAI may use images to improve their services (subject to OpenAI's terms)
  • No personal identification information is sent with images to OpenAI
  • All data is encrypted in transit using industry-standard encryption
  • Images are automatically deleted from our temporary storage within 1 hour

Data Sharing and Social Features

User-Controlled Sharing

  • You decide: All sharing of styled images is entirely under your control
  • Social media: You may choose to share styled images on social platforms using iOS sharing features
  • Responsibility: You are solely responsible for any images you choose to share publicly

No Automatic Sharing

  • We never automatically share your images
  • We do not have access to your social media accounts
  • All sharing requires your explicit action

Data Security

iOS Security Features

  • All sensitive data stored using iOS Keychain with hardware encryption
  • Network communications use TLS/HTTPS encryption
  • App follows Apple's security best practices

Local Storage

  • Images stored in your device's secure photo library
  • No cloud backup of image data by our app
  • Standard iOS backup policies apply to your device

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

  • Our app is not directed to children under 13
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

Your Rights

  • Right to Know: You can request information about data collection practices
  • Right to Delete: You can request deletion of personal information (contact us)
  • Right to Opt-Out: You can opt-out by uninstalling the app
  • Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights

Data Sales

  • We do not sell personal information to third parties
  • We do not share personal information for monetary or other valuable consideration

International Users (GDPR)

Legal Basis for Processing

  • Consent: For optional features and permissions
  • Legitimate Interest: For core app functionality and improvement
  • Contract: For providing requested services

Your Rights

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data
  • Objection: Object to certain types of processing

Data Retention

Automatic Deletion

  • Authentication tokens: Deleted when you log out or uninstall the app
  • Original user images: Automatically deleted within 1 hour after processing
  • AI processing prompts: Stored permanently for service functionality
  • Generated/styled images: Stored permanently on our servers until user requests deletion
  • Purchase data: Retained according to Apple's policies and legal requirements (typically 4-6 years for tax purposes)
  • Subscription information: Maintained while subscription is active and for legal compliance periods
  • Credit transactions: Retained for account management and fraud prevention (typically 2-3 years)
  • Usage data: Processed and aggregated, individual records deleted regularly (30-90 days)
  • Device information: Deleted upon app uninstallation
  • Network logs: Automatically deleted after 7 days

User Control

  • You can delete the app at any time to remove most associated data
  • You can request deletion of your generated images by contacting us
  • Styled images remain in your photo library under your control
  • You can delete individual images from your photo library
  • You can request complete account deletion with all associated data
  • You can cancel subscriptions through Apple's subscription management

  • Significant changes will be communicated through the app

  • Check this policy periodically for changes
