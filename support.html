Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to iPhone Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → AItist → Cancel Subscription
How do credits work?
Credits are used to process images with AI. Each image transformation uses credits. You can buy credits or get them with a subscription.
Are my photos private?
Yes! Your original photos are automatically deleted within 1 hour after processing. We never share your images.
How do I get a refund?
We do not process refunds directly. All purchases are final and handled through Apple's App Store. For refund requests, contact Apple Support directly.
Can I use images commercially?
Yes! You own your images and can use them for personal or commercial purposes.
